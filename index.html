Naruto's desperate attempt to bring back his friend, leading to their first major battle at the Valley of the End.

The devastating attack on Konoha by Pain, where Naruto finally gains the village's acknowledgment.

The greatest conflict in shinobi history, uniting all villages against Madara and the Ten-Tails.
